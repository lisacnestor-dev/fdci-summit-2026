# FDCI Stanford Summit 2026 — Conference Website

## Project Overview
A standalone conference website for the FDCI Stanford Summit, a half-day event at Stanford University in late July 2026. Organized by Lisa Nestor, Research Director at the Stanford Future of Digital Currency Initiative (FDCI).

## Event Details
- **Event:** FDCI Stanford Summit 2026
- **Format:** Half-day summit (morning), research presentations + roundtable
- **Date:** Late July 2026 (exact date TBD)

## Sections (in order)
1. **Hero** — bold tagline, date/location/format, CTA
2. **Speakers** — card grid with photo, name, title, affiliation
3. **Sessions** — 3–4 research sessions + closing roundtable
4. **Details** — date, location, audience, format
